Our Story

EEG is essential yet challenging: manual reviews often take 40–45 minutes per case, expert agreement can be just 78–83%, and data is fragmented across 170,000+ signals in non-standard formats that are hard to integrate with hospital systems.

White Neuron was founded to change this. We built an AI-assisted EEG platform that turns complex signals into standardized, interoperable insights — reducing turnaround to ~4 minutes, improving accuracy to 94–98%, and enabling seamless integration with HIS/PACS and automated DICOM conversion. Our mission is simple: help clinicians make faster, safer decisions for every patient.
